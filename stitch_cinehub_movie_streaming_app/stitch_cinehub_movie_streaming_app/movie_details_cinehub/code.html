Kantara

A story that explores the complex relationship between humanity and nature, deeply rooted in the folklore and myths of a coastal village in Karnataka.

Description

When greed paves the way for betrayal, scheming and murder, a young tribal reluctantly dons the traditions of his ancestors to seek justice. The film explores the conflict between the forest officials and the local community, woven with the supernatural elements of Bhoota Kola.
